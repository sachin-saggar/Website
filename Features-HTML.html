USBFree Features

The Urban Sciences building is relatively new, and visitors may struggle to find their way around it, or students may struggle to find specific offices. This app is designed with this in mind, and will make navigation around the building a lot less of a hassle.

Search for staff members and offices

Easily find the office or staff member you are looking for with a special search feature.

Maps of each floor and navigation

Need to know your way around each floor? A floor plan for each floor will be provided to help guide you. Additionally, you can easily navigate to your desired office - type in the office code or staff member and the app will helpfully provide a route.

Get updates from the School of Computing

Synchronize the app to Newcastle University's Facebook and Twitter feed to get the latest updates from the School of Computing and the Urban Sciences Building.
