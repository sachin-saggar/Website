What is the Urban Sciences Building?

The Urban Sciences Building, or USB for short, is a new facility at Newcastle University which is designed for students in the School of Computing.

Can I book a room?

USBFree currently does not provide any means of being able to book a room. This is because we do not have the capabilities to be able to book a room directly, and neither do any of the students who study Computing. Furthermore, requests will need to be taken through the Computing reception, which is impossible in the app alone.

What are the maps for?

They are floor plans for the building. They should help you easily find your way around.

Does this app work on iPhone?

At the current time of writing, there is no iPhone support because of lack of compatibility.
